Rule and Regulation

1. General Rules

  • All employees must adhere to the company's code of conduct.
  • Employees should maintain a professional and respectful work environment.
  • Confidential information must be kept secure and not disclosed to unauthorized parties.
  • Employees are expected to be punctual and maintain regular attendance.

2. Dress Code

  • Employees should dress in a professional and appropriate manner.
  • Casual wear, such as shorts and flip-flops, is not permitted.
  • Employees must maintain a neat and well-groomed appearance.

3. Workplace Safety

  • Employees must follow all safety guidelines and procedures.
  • Any accidents or incidents must be reported immediately to the supervisor.
  • Employees should maintain a clean and organized work area.

4. Employee Conduct

  • Employees should treat colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with respect and professionalism.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or any form of misconduct will not be tolerated.
  • Employees should use company resources responsibly and for business purposes only.
